Azalea Town

"A town where people and Pokémon live together in simple harmony."

Hidden Items
  • Full Heal: Walk behind the Slowpoke Well and check the center brick of the northern wall of the well to receive a Full Heal.
Fun Facts
  • Azalea Town is home to the Slowpoke Well! As such, Slowpoke can be found around the town.
Important Buildings
  • Kurt's House: A custom Poké Ball creator who makes Poké Balls out of Apricorns. He lives on the northwest part of town.
  • Azalea Gym: Defeat Bug-type gym leader Bugsy to win the Hive Badge!
